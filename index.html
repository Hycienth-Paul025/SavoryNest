Frequently Asked Questions

"Find quick answers to some of our most common guest inquiries."
From reservations to special requests, here’s everything you need to know.
Got questions? We’ve got answers.

Do you offer online reservations?

Yes! You can easily reserve a table through our website using the “Reserve a Table” button.

Can I order food for delivery or pickup?

Absolutely. We partner with top delivery services to bring our dishes to your doorstep, or you can schedule a pickup directly from us.

Do you cater for private events?

Yes, we offer catering for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Contact us for a tailored menu.

Are your ingredients locally sourced?

Most of our ingredients come from trusted local farms to ensure freshness and support the community.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

We have a variety of vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free meals. Please let us know your dietary needs when ordering.

How can I become a sponsor or partner?

Simply reach out via our “Contact” page, and our team will get in touch with partnership opportunities.
